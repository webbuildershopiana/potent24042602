Founding Story
Potent Wellness Solutions LLP, under the brand Potent Hygiene, was born from the compassionate
insights of founders Divivta Agarwal and Niraj Jain.
This story begins with Divita, whose experiences as a woman in India deeply influenced her path and
ignited a passion to revolutionize feminine hygiene. Growing up in a traditional society, she observed
first-hand the stigma and silence surrounding womenâ€™s health and menstrual hygiene. Conversations
about menstruation were often whispered, if they happened at all, and the lack of proper sanitary
products led many women, including those in her community, to use unsafe materials. These
practices were not just out dated but posed severe health risks and perpetuated a cycle of shame
and inconvenience around natural biological processes. While volunteering in rural outreach
programs, she encountered the harsh realities that women faced due to the unavailability of proper
menstrual care products. Stories of young girls missing school during their periods and elderly
women suffering in silence struck a chord with her. The inequity was glaring, and the environmental
toll of non-biodegradable hygiene products added another layer of urgency.
Vision
At Potent Wellness Solutions LLP, we envision a world where every individual has access to
premium, sustainable personal hygiene products. We aim to empower people globally, especially
women and vulnerable populations, by providing innovative solutions that enhance health, promote environmental stewardship, and uphold dignity. Our vision is to lead a global movement that
celebrates and supports the natural health needs of all individuals, making eco-friendly personal
care universally available and accepted, thus fostering a healthier, more sustainable relationship
Mission Statement
Our mission is to redefine personal hygiene by delivering innovative, sustainable, and highly effective products that cater to the diverse needs of global populations. We are dedicated to:
Developing Breakthrough Products
Introducing advanced personal hygiene solutions that combine cutting-edge technology with sustainable practices. Our focus is on creating products that offer exceptional performance, comfort, and reliability, such as our rash-free sanitary pads and high-efficiency menstrual cups.
Promoting Sustainable Practices
Committing to environmental excellence by integrating green
manufacturing processes, renewable materials, and waste-reduction methodologies. Our goal is to
minimize our ecological footprint while maximizing the health benefits to our users.
Enhancing Global Accessibility
Ensuring that our eco-friendly personal hygiene products are
affordable and accessible to people in various geographic and socio-economic settings. We strive to
break down barriers to access and foster inclusivity in personal care.
Empowering Through Education
Leading robust educational initiatives that inform and engage
communities on the importance of good menstrual hygiene practices and sustainable living. We
partner with global NGOs to provide workshops and resources that empower individuals, especially
women and girls, to take charge of their health with confidence and knowledge.
Upholding Ethical Standards
Operating with the utmost integrity and transparency. We maintain
clear, honest communication with our customers about the sourcing, composition, and
environmental impact of our products to build trust and encourage informed consumer choices."
