Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What types of products does Potent Hygiene offer?

Potent Hygiene offers a variety of eco-friendly personal hygiene products including biodegradable
menstrual cups, biodegradable sanitary pads, reusable pee funnels, and biodegradable paper toilet
seat covers. Each product is designed to offer a sustainable alternative to traditional hygiene
products, with a focus on women's health and environmental responsibility.

How do I use a Potent Hygiene menstrual cup?

To use the menstrual cup, fold it using one of the recommended folding techniques (such as the C-
fold or Punch-down fold). Gently insert the folded cup into your vagina, allowing it to open and
create a seal. The cup can be worn for up to 12 hours, depending on your flow. To remove, gently
pull on the stem to break the seal, then pull out the cup, empty it, rinse, and reinsert if needed.

What are the benefits of using biodegradable sanitary pads?

Our organic sanitary pads are made from hypoallergenic, skin-friendly materials that are gentle on
the skin and highly absorbent. They are free from chemicals, dyes, and fragrances, making them
ideal for women with sensitive skin or allergies. Additionally, they are environmentally friendly, as
they are made from organic materials that are biodegradable.

How do I use the reusable pee funnel?

Hold the pee funnel against your body with the wider end positioned securely under the flow area.
Ensure it is snug against your body to prevent leaks. Tilt the funnel slightly downwards to direct the
flow away from your feet. After use, rinse the funnel with water and soap, dry, and store it in its
cotton pouch.

Can the pee funnel be used during menstruation?

Yes, the pee funnel can be used during menstruation as an aid for urination while standing. It's
particularly useful when you're using a public restroom and want to avoid contact with toilet seats. It
does not interfere with the use of menstrual cups or tampons.

Are Potent Hygiene products suitable for all ages?

Yes, our products are designed to accommodate the needs of women of all ages. From young teens
who are new to menstruation to older women, our hygiene products offer comfort, reliability, and
ease of use.

How do I care for and maintain my Potent Hygiene products?

After each use, menstrual cups should be rinsed and sterilized before storage. Sanitary pads can be
disposed of responsibly. Pee funnels should be washed with soap and water and dried thoroughly
before storing in their pouch. Always follow the specific care instructions provided with each
product to ensure longevity and effectiveness.

Are Potent Hygiene products travel-friendly?

Yes, all our products are compact and designed to be discreet for easy travel. Menstrual cups come
with a carrying case, and pee funnels include a cotton pouch for convenient storage and transport,
making them ideal for travelers who want to maintain their hygiene routines on the go.

What is your policy on returns?

Due to the hygienic nature of our products, we cannot accept returns once the packaging is opened.
This policy ensures the safety and integrity of our products for all customers.

What should I do if I experience discomfort or issues with a product?

If you experience any discomfort or issues while using our products, we recommend discontinuing
use immediately and consulting a healthcare professional if necessary. For specific product inquiries
or concerns, please contact our customer service for assistance and further guidance.
